Bruno Mars

About

Bruno Mars is an American singer-songwriter. He is known for his stage performances, retro showmanship, and for singing in a wide range of musical styles, including pop, R&B, funk, soul, reggae, disco, and rock.

Bruno Mars has many accomplishes as a singer songwriter. He is very popular currently and first started his career in 2010. After his first hit song he became very famous.
